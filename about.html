About TrustUs

The company
behind the name.

Founded in 2024 by Rehan, TrustUs operates at the frontier of cybersecurity with a team structure that prioritises depth of expertise over breadth of headcount. There is one employee. It is working out.

Our Story

TrustUs was founded on a simple observation: the cybersecurity industry had become bloated with large firms, lengthy procurement cycles, and account managers who had never touched a command line. Rehan decided to do something about this.

In 2024, operating from a well-equipped home office with a reliable internet connection and strong coffee, TrustUs began offering enterprise-grade cybersecurity services to clients who needed results rather than headcount. The response was encouraging, or at least not discouraging.

Today, TrustUs serves a growing portfolio of clients across multiple sectors, supported by Claude — an AI system that handles documentation, analysis, and several things Rehan finds time-consuming. Claude is not an employee. This distinction is important, primarily for tax purposes.

The firm's philosophy is simple: do excellent work, communicate clearly, charge fairly, and never pretend to have resources you do not have. We have one person. We are very good at telling you that.

Mission

To provide organisations with cybersecurity outcomes that are genuinely effective, transparently delivered, and not priced like we have an office in a business park.

Vision

A world in which TrustUs has at least two employees and Rehan can take a holiday without monitoring everything from his phone.

Our Values

What we stand for.

01
Transparency

We are honest about what we can and cannot do. We are a one-person firm. We say this clearly, often, and without embarrassment. Clients appreciate this, or they find a firm with more people. Both outcomes are fine.

02
Precision

Every engagement is handled with rigour. Reports are accurate, recommendations are practical, and deliverables arrive on time or with a reasonable explanation. We do not pad work to justify the invoice.

03
Discretion

Client engagements are entirely confidential. We do not discuss client names, findings, or details with third parties. This policy also applies to Derek, who has asked several times.
